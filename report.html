Crime, sadly, will never go away. But, with the use of Philly Lookout, we could at least try to stop it in its tracks. In the United States, a house is burgled on average, every 13 seconds. 70% of these burglaries are committed by someone in the local area. And 80% of the burglars will burgle another house within 7 days. Astonishing stats which describe the extent of the problem. Philly Lookout mimics a social network app. Instead of posting social updates, you and other users can post about local crimes and what you see. Posting about local crimes will inform the neighbourhood to be vigilant.

Have you seen an incident or something suspicious? Do you want your neighbors and fellow Philadelphians to know about? Report what you saw, give your name (you can submit as anonymous), tell us the location of the incident/crime, and a description of what you saw or what happened.

Stay safe!
